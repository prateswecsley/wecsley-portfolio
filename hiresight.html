Common Questions

Things HR teams
always ask us.

Do we have to replace our current ATS?+

No. HireSight connects to your ATS via bidirectional API — reads your candidates, writes scores back into your pipeline. Your system of record stays intact. Setup takes under 24 hours for most teams.

What counts as one resume analysis?+

Each analysis type uses 1 credit. A full analysis (AI scoring + authenticity check) uses 2 credits per candidate. Your monthly pool is completely flexible — use all of it on one role or spread across any number of positions.

How is this different from what our ATS already does?+

Most ATS tools rank and sort candidates — they don't explain why. HireSight adds written reasoning per candidate, segmentation by role type, dimension-by-dimension authenticity breakdown, and interview question generation. It's the layer between "ranked list" and "hiring decision."

How does candidate data stay secure?+

Candidate data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We do not use candidate data to train AI models. Role-based access control limits who sees what. HireSight is built for GDPR and CCPA compliance from day one.

Is it compliant with AI hiring laws?+

Yes. HireSight's explainable scoring engine satisfies NYC Local Law 144 and EU AI Act Annex III requirements — both requiring transparent, auditable AI decisions in hiring. Bias audit reports are included on Professional and Enterprise plans.

What happens if we go over our monthly limit?+

Overages are charged at $0.10 per additional analysis. We'll alert you when you reach 80% of your monthly pool so you're never caught off guard. You can also pause job postings until the next billing cycle.
